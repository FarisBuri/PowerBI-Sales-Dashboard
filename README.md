# PowerBI-Sales-Dashboard

# 📊 Power BI Sales Analysis Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **Sales Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI**, supported by SQL data modeling and transformation.

The goal is to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and product insights to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI (Data Visualization)
* SQL (Data Modeling & Transformation)
* Excel / CSV (Data Source)

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset includes:

* Customers information
* Products and pricing
* Promotions and discounts
* Sales transactions

---

## 📊 Key Features

### 🔹 Sales Insights

* Monthly Net Sales trend
* Comparison between Q1–Q2 and Q3–Q4
* Growth rate analysis (+20%)

### 🔹 KPI Overview

* Total Net Sales: 122.31M
* Total Profit: 31.66M
* Total Orders: 3.51K
* Total Discount: 20K

### 🔹 Customer Insights

* Average Sales per Customer
* Customer distribution by state
* Top & Bottom performing regions

### 🔹 Product Analysis

* Top performing products by profit
* Bottom products analysis
* Identification of low-value products

---

## 💡 Business Insights

* Some products have high demand but low profitability
* Sales peak in Q3 and Q4 compared to Q1 and Q2
* Customer distribution varies significantly by region

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

Final Insight:
Sales growth driven by high-value products
Low-performing products need marketing focus
🔹 Business Impact:
Opportunity to increase revenue via bundling
